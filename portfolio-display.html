About the Project

To start my UX/UI freelancing business, I began by creating a minimal portfolio site to showcase my work without distractions but still with a bit of flair. I designed two overlapping circles in the hero section for visual interest and added the usual elements like headings and a CTA button. Below that, I displayed screenshots of past projects and used the images as buttons linking to detailed project pages. I wrapped up the page with a pricing section and a footer that matches one of the hero circles and includes my email and copyright.
